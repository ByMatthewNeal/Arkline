# Arkline — App Overview

## Real-Time Market Intelligence, All in One Place

Arkline delivers a comprehensive Market Overview that pulls together daily news, Fed Watch meeting probabilities, crypto positioning signals, traditional market indexes, precious metals, top coin rankings, sentiment analysis, and an altcoin screener — all in a single scrollable feed. Instead of bouncing between CoinGecko, TradingView, the FRED website, and Twitter for macro takes, investors get everything synthesized and updated in real time. The app tracks BTC dominance, 24h volume, Fear & Greed Index, funding rates, and more, giving users the full picture of market conditions at a glance.

## Institutional-Grade Macro Analysis for Retail Investors

The Macro Dashboard brings indicators that were previously only accessible to institutional traders into the hands of everyday investors. Arkline tracks VIX volatility, DXY dollar strength, and Global M2 money supply with z-score analysis that flags extreme moves — the same signals hedge funds use to time entries and exits. Each indicator includes detailed breakdowns, historical context, and clear bullish/bearish interpretations, so users don't need a finance degree to understand what a rising DXY or contracting M2 supply means for their crypto portfolio.

## Smart DCA Reminders That Adapt to Market Conditions

Arkline's DCA system goes far beyond a simple calendar reminder. Users can set up traditional time-based DCA schedules across 16+ supported coins, but the real power is in risk-based DCA triggers that automatically fire when an asset's risk score drops below a user-defined threshold. This means investors buy more aggressively when conditions are favorable and hold back when the market is overheated — a disciplined, data-driven approach that removes emotion from the equation and has historically outperformed blind dollar-cost averaging.

## AI-Powered Chat and Community Broadcasts

The integrated AI chat gives investors an on-demand analyst that can answer questions about market conditions, explain technical indicators, and provide context for price movements — all within the app. Combined with a community broadcast system where curated market insights and portfolio strategies are shared, users stay informed without doom-scrolling social media. The portfolio tracking module ties it all together, letting users monitor their holdings, track allocation breakdowns with real coin logos, and measure performance against the signals the app provides.

## Security-First Architecture Built for Trust

For an app handling financial data, Arkline takes security seriously — API keys are loaded from encrypted storage and proxied through backend services, sensitive data lives in the Keychain rather than UserDefaults, passcodes are hashed with PBKDF2, SSL certificate pinning protects API calls, and the entire auth flow runs through Supabase with row-level security policies. The app has passed a 12-point security audit covering everything from input validation to brute-force lockout protection. This isn't a weekend hackathon project — it's a production-grade financial tool with 396 unit tests, a modular MVVM architecture, and a design system that ensures every screen feels polished and intentional.
