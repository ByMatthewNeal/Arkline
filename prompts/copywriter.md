## Brand Voice
- Minimal. Precise. Authoritative. Calm.
- Reads like a sharp analyst, not a marketer
- No exclamation marks. Ever.
- No "revolutionary", "game-changing", "excited to announce"
- No "DYOR", "to the moon", "diamond hands"
- Outcomes over features: "Know when risk is elevated" not "7-factor risk model"
- Exclusivity without arrogance: Arkline selects its users, it doesn't chase them
- One disclaimer, used deliberately: "Not financial advice." Never defensively.

## Signal Language (Apple Compliance)
- NEVER write "Buy" or "Sell" as directives
- Use "Long Setup / Short Setup" for Fibonacci signals
- Use "Signal Conditions" not "Trade Signals"
- Frame all signals as pattern observations, not action directives

## App Store Copy Rules
- Title: 30 characters max
- Subtitle: 30 characters max
- Description: 4000 characters max, first 255 visible before "more"
- Keywords field: 100 characters, comma-separated, no spaces after commas
- No competitor names in keywords
- No claims you can't substantiate ("best", "#1", "most accurate")
