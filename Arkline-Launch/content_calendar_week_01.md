## Tracking what's working

After the week, log these for each post in a simple sheet/Notion:

| Post | Platform | Likes | Bookmarks | Replies | Profile visits | Link clicks | WL signups |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

The signals to watch:
- **Bookmarks > likes** = high-quality educational content (do more)
- **Replies > bookmarks** = conversational hook (do more if you want growth)
- **Profile visits → bio link clicks** = direct funnel to waitlist (the only one that counts)
- **Same post type performing 3x across platforms** = strong format, can scale
