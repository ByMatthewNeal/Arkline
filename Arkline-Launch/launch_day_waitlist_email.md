# Launch Day: Waitlist Email

**When to send:** Same time you push "Release This Version" in App Store Connect. Timing tip: do this in the morning your time — better open rates and gives you the full day to respond to replies.

**Send to:** Your full waitlist (Supabase `early_access` table, or wherever the list lives — Loops, ConvertKit, etc.).

**From:** matt@arkline.io (or mneal.jw@gmail.com if you're sending via personal Gmail for max deliverability).

**Format:** Plain text. Copy the body below into your email tool as-is — do NOT reformat with bold, headers, or HTML styling. The plain-text feel is the point.

---

## Subject line options

Pick one. Test if your tool supports it.

- A: `It's live.`
- B: `Arkline is on the App Store`
- C: `Day one. 150 founding spots open.`

---

## Body

```
Hey,

It's live.

Arkline just went live on the App Store. If you
signed up early, you're first in line for
founding member pricing — $39.99/mo or $399.99/yr,
locked forever as long as you stay subscribed. Only
150 spots at this price, then it goes to $69.99/mo.

Download here:
https://apps.apple.com/app/arkline-market-intelligence/id6760355430

Two things worth saying up front:

- Thank you for waiting. Review took longer than
  I wanted. If you signed up months ago and were
  starting to think this wasn't real — it is, and
  I appreciate your patience.

- Everything I promised in the earlier emails is
  in the app. BTC risk score. Macro dashboard.
  Smart DCA. Morning and evening AI briefings.
  Portfolio tracking across 20,000+ assets.
  No tiers, no upsells — one price, everything
  included.

If you subscribe and something's broken, confusing,
or missing, hit reply. This inbox goes to me, and
I read every response. The best product feedback
I've gotten so far has come from this list.

Talk soon,
Matt
Founder, Arkline

P.S. Founding pricing is grandfathered. If you
subscribe in the first 150 and stay subscribed,
you pay $39.99 forever — even after we raise the
price. That's the deal.
```

---

## Notes

- Keep line breaks exactly as-is. The hand-typed feel is the point.
- If sending via Loops / ConvertKit / other ESP: use the plain-text template, not HTML.
- If sending via personal Gmail: paste as plain text (Gmail sometimes tries to auto-format — check the composer says "Plain text mode").
- Do NOT add a company logo header, footer social icons, or an unsubscribe design flourish. Standard footer (unsubscribe link, physical address for CAN-SPAM) can be plain-text as well.
- Voice reference: matches `/Arkline/Arkline-Launch/email_sequence_drafts.md` house style.
